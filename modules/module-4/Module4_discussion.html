Module 4 Discussion: Misleading Visualizations

Due: Initial post Thursday | Responses Sunday

Initial Post

Share the most misleading visualization you found or created this week:

  1. Describe or share the visualization — What does it appear to show?
  2. What makes it misleading? — Truncated axis? Cherry-picked timeframe? Missing context? Visual tricks?
  3. How could someone spot the problem? — What should a critical viewer look for?

You can share visualizations from the "Make It Lie" exercise, AI-generated charts, or examples you found online.

Response Posts

Respond to at least 2 classmates by Sunday:

  • Would you have spotted the misleading element without their explanation?
  • Have you seen similar techniques used elsewhere?
