Module 5: Communication & Synthesis

Week 5 of 6 | Estimated time: ~16 hours

Big Question: How do we communicate technical findings clearly and effectively?

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Evaluate technical explanations for clarity and accuracy
  • Identify common problems in AI-generated explanations
  • Document methodology clearly enough for reproduction
  • Audit your AI tool usage and make informed privacy decisions

Major Assessment: AI Privacy & Security Audit (25% of grade)

Audit your AI tool usage, evaluate privacy policies using the course framework, and make informed decisions about your data. Document what you found, what you changed, and what trade-offs you made.
