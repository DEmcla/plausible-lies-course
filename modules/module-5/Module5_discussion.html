Module 5 Discussion: Technical Writing Clarity

Due: Initial post Thursday | Responses Sunday

Initial Post

What makes technical writing clear vs. confusing? Share examples:

  1. Share a GOOD example — A clear technical explanation you've encountered (can be from this course, a help document, tutorial, etc.)
  2. Share a BAD example — A confusing technical explanation
  3. What makes the difference? — Identify 2-3 specific things that distinguish clear from confusing technical writing

Response Posts

Respond to at least 2 classmates by Sunday:

  • Do you agree with their assessment of what makes writing clear?
  • Would you add any other principles for clear technical communication?
