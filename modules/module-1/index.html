Module 1: Foundations

Week 1 of 6 | Estimated time: ~16 hours

Big Question: What is AI, really? How do different types of AI models work, and why does that matter for how we evaluate them?

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Explain how LLMs work as word prediction systems (not search engines or thinking machines)
  • Understand why AI "hallucinates" and why confidence doesn't indicate accuracy
  • Distinguish between different types of AI models (LLMs, image generators, classifiers, recommendation systems)
  • Match AI model types to appropriate tasks
  • Set up your learning portfolio document with proper structure
