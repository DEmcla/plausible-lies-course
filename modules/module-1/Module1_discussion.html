Module 1 Discussion: Introductions & Mental Models

Due: Initial post Thursday | Responses Sunday

Initial Post

Introduce yourself to the class and reflect on your prior understanding of AI:

  1. Introduce yourself briefly — Name, major/program, one interesting thing about you
  2. Share one AI tool you use regularly (or have tried) — What do you use it for?
  3. Before this week's readings, how did you think AI worked? — What mental model did you have? Did you think of it as a search engine, a thinking machine, something else?

This is exploratory, not evaluative. There are no "wrong" answers about what you thought before. The goal is to notice how our mental models change as we learn.

Response Posts

Respond to at least 2 classmates by Sunday:

  • Did you share similar assumptions about how AI works?
  • What surprised you most about how AI actually works?
