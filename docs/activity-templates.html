Benchmark BS Detection Week 4

Purpose: Critically evaluate AI benchmark claims.

Benchmark Selected

Source (with link)
What claim is being made?

e.g., "Model X outperforms Model Y on task Z"

Critical Evaluation

Question Finding
Who conducted the benchmark?
Is there potential conflict of interest?
What exactly was measured?
Does the benchmark reflect real-world use?
Is data contamination possible?
Are error bars or confidence intervals shown?

Issues Identified

List at least two potential problems with this benchmark

Conclusion

How much should you trust this benchmark claim? Why?
