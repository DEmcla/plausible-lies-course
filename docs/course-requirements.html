Course Requirements

Software

Microsoft Word (Office 365 / Microsoft 365)

This course requires Microsoft Word from Microsoft 365 (formerly Office 365). You will use Word throughout the semester to build your learning portfolio, developing professional document skills including styles, tables of contents, and section formatting.

  • MCLA students: You have free access to Microsoft 365 through your student account. Download the desktop apps at portal.office.com using your MCLA credentials.
  • Non-MCLA students: If you do not have access to Microsoft 365, contact the instructor during the first week of class.

Note: The free web version of Word (office.com) does not support all features required for this course. You need the desktop application. Older versions of Word (2019 and earlier) may work but are not supportedâ€”if your interface looks different from course materials, you're on your own.

Hardware

You need access to a computer (not just a phone or tablet) to complete coursework. Campus computer labs are available if you don't have a personal computer.

Canvas

All course materials, assignments, and submissions are managed through Canvas. You'll need reliable internet access to participate in the course.

No Textbook

There is no required textbook for this course. All readings will be provided through Canvas or linked online.

Questions?
